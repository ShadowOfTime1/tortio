# Tortio 🍰

Recipe scaling for cake makers — by size, by weight, by tier.

## What it does

- Scale a cake recipe to any diameter or weight — ingredients rescale automatically.
- Multi-tier cakes: each tier has its own size.
- Three scale modes: by volume (sponge, cream), by area (icing, syrup), fixed (decor).
- Local-first: your recipes live on your device. Optional Google Drive backup.
- Free. No ads. No tracking. No subscriptions.
